== Description ==

Attach reviews and place multi-ratings to any post on your site including WooCommerce products.

The plugin has lots of features, including:
- Responsive layout
- WooCommerce compatible
- Multi-ratings
- Review comments and votes
- AJAX sorting
- Submit review form
- Customizable

Look at our [demo](https://www.salephpscripts.com/wordpress-ratings-reviews/demo)
